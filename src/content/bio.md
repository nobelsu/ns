### HEYY! 😝 How r u doinnn!
Lemme tell you about myself. I'm a proud Indonesian 🇮🇩 🇮🇩 🇮🇩 !! I was born in the city of Medan, which is located in the northern region of Sumatera island. My first language was NOT English. In fact, my parents spoke some mix of Indonesian, Chinese, the Hokkien dialect. On my first day of nursery school, I bawled my eyes out because I couldn't a single word anyone was saying. After all, my grandparents would say

> "Nobel ah, 你 kemaren *jia* *liau* *bui*" 

which roughly translates to "Nobel, have you eaten?" in like three different languages LOL. One could imagine the horrors a 3-year-old me had to face when he heard all that English gibberish 😵‍💫

That aside! My two fav things in the world are KPOP and Japanese literature. Contrary to popular belief, I got into K-Pop NOT because of the idols, but because my mum used to blast BIGBANG and iKON while I did my math homework 🤣 These days, I'm the one now playing ILLIT or ADP on loop to annoying degrees at times. Meanwhile, reading is relatively recent for me. I've only started in the last few months, but I've become a BIGGG Murakami fan (Kafka on the Shore FTWW)
